BMW is a German vehicle business that was formed in 1916. It also owns and manufactures Mini automobiles and Rolls-Royce Motor Cars. BMW is one of the world's top three luxury automakers.

Ferrari is an Italian automobile company that competes in the Formula One World Championship as well as producing high-end and high-performance racing vehicles, supercars, and luxury sports cars.

Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A. is an Italian brand and producer of premium supercars, sports vehicles, and SUVs located in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy, as well as tractors Lamborghini Trattori.

Porsche is a German automaker known for its high-performance and premium sports vehicles, SUVs, and sedans. Ferdinand Porsche founded Porsche in 1931 as a design engineering department.

Suzuki Motor Corporation is a Japanese manufacturer that manufactures a variety of tiny autos, motorbikes, outboard motors, and other small combustion-powered engine items.

Toyota Motor Corporation is a Japanese multinational automobile manufacturer based in Toyota, Aichi, Japan. As of December 2019, was the world's tenth-largest firm in terms of revenue.
